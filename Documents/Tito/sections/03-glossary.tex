\section{Glossary}\label{sec:3.0}

\begin{description}[style=nextline,leftmargin=1em,labelindent=0pt,font=\sffamily\bfseries]
  \item[Anti-Guerrilla Operation] A special form of combat that allows Axis
  units to enter Yugoslav Mountains and Hide-aways and attack at more
  advantageous ratios.
  \item[Axis] The Axis Player controls German, Italian, Croat, Serbian,
  Bulgarian (before Game-Turn 15), and sometimes Chetnik units.
  \item[Combat Strength] A quantification of a unit's strength in attack and defense.
  \item[Guerrilla] A Partisan or Chetnik unit.
  \item[Objective Display] A rectangle, divided into two boxes of equal size,
  representing key cities, towns, market towns, villages, and industrial sites.
  \item[Occupation Zone] One of the 12 areas representing the historical zones
  delineated by the Axis High Command in 1941.
  \item[Yugoslav] The Yugoslav Player controls Partisan, Soviet, Bulgarian
  (after Game-Turn 14), and sometimes Chetnik units.
  \item[Zone Display] A collection of three triangles (``Mountain triangles'')
  and three circles (``Hide-away circles'') belonging to one Occupation Zone.
\end{description}
