\subsection{ARTILLERY BOMBARDMENT AND DUELS}

Artillery, having a much longer range than any other type of unit, has some special capabilities. These rules apply to both Artillery and Horse Artillery units, both referred to here as "Artillery".

\begin{enumerate}[label=(\alph*)]
  \item An attacking player may, instead of making a normal attack with all his "Committed" Combat Factors, choose to use only his artillery Combat Factors.

  \item If this is done, the attacker says that he will conduct a "bombardment".

  \item During a bombardment, Combat is conducted normally, but only the Combat Factors of the opposing artillery units are used; the other "Committed" units take no part in the Combat. If the defender has no artillery, he can do no harm to the attacker, and only the attacker will roll a die for results on the COMBAT EFFECTS TABLES.

  \item All possible eliminated Combat Factors from a bombardment should be Artillery Combat Factors.

  \item If a "Disorder" result is rolled, this will effet all "Committed" units, not just the artillery.

  \item Artillery can conduct a bombardment across a stream or river hexside while in Battle Formation.
\end{enumerate}
