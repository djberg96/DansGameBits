\subsection{GARRISONS}

Garrisons are troops that are assigned to defend a specific point. They are expectedd to remain at, or very near, the points they are assigned to defend, and are not expected to take part in field operations.

\begin{enumerate}
  \item Units listed as being garrison troops in the various scenarios must remain in or within two hexes of the point they are assigned to at all times. If forced to retreat further than this distance, they are assumed to be eliminated (Note that they can retreat off the Mapboard without this result if the edge is two hexes or less away from their assigned point.)

  \item B\&O garrison can operate anywhere along the B\&O RR line, or within two hexes of it.

  \item The Union Player must take care not to mix his garrison and field units, so as not to move the garrison units off with field troops who may be temporarily close or in the same hex.
\end{enumerate}

\textbf{Note:} Garrison units can be withdrawn from the Mapboard, but they cannot be counted in this way for reducing the Union Resource Factor.
