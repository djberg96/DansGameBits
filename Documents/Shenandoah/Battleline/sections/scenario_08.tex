\begin{scenariodata}
\textbf{PLAYTESTER'S COMMENTS:} The Confederates must heavily guard\\
\end{scenariodata}

their wagons to protect them, and screen them with their combat units. They must get across the Shenandoah River as quickly as possible, then head for Winchester. Keep a road south open so that the supply wagons can move that way by game's end (don't overdo this and leave the forces around Winchester unsupplied!). Avoid attacks, as these will use up supplies. In order for the Union Player to win, they must take some risks in heading the Confederates off from Winchester, then hang on while Wright, Emory and Crook arrive for a showdown battle.
