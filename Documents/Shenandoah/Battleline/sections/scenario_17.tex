\section{SCENARIO \#17: "1862"
}


Players play in succession Scenarios \#1, \#2, \#3, \#4, and \#5. After each scenario, score as follows:

1. The side with the larger number of total Victory Points is awarded a number of Victory Points equal to the difference in Victory Point totals. For instance, if a Scenario ended with the Union having 61

\subsection*{Victory Points and the Confederates 49 Victory Points, the Union}

would be awarded 12 Victory Points towards winning the game.

2. A side which wins a Scenario is awarded 50 Victory Points, in addition to any differences in the Victory Point totals.

These Victory Points are totaled up after all five Scenarios have been played. To win, a side must have at least 50 Victory Points total more than the other side for ail five Scenarios. Any other result is a draw.
