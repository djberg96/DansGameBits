The basic ''short"' Scenarios are being played, but, since they are part of a longer series, they must be evaluated and played somewhat differently than normal. In Scenarios where the Player has the advantage, he should play his advantage to the hilt, running up his Victory Point totals as high as possible. In Scenarios where the Player is at a disadvantage, it may be advantageous to take as few risks as possible and play for a drawn game.

VARIATION ON VICTORY CONDITIONS: Players play the same Scenario twice in succession, switching sides for the second game. Each Player totals up the Victory Points he acquired during the two games, and the Player with the larger total is the winner.
